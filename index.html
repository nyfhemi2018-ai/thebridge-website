How FIC Work

The safety rails behind Africa’s new trading ecosystem.

Step 1

Trader Evaluation & Onboarding

We begin by assessing each trader’s experience, trading style, and risk profile to build a personalized safety structure.

Step 2

Licensing & Risk Structuring

Traders receive their unique Trader License Number (TLN) and a tailored risk discipline framework to prevent emotional and financial breakdowns.

Step 3

AI Risk Monitoring & Alert

Our AI monitors volatility, exposure, and behavioral signals—providing real-time alerts before losses spiral out of control.

Protection, Support & Growth

FIC acts as a safety net for traders and brokers with dispute support, assurance, and long-term growth visibility.
